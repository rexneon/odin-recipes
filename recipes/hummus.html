Hummus

Description

Hummus is a relatively new product on the market and it was first created by accident. According to the legend, a steam roller lost control of its brakes one fateful morning just as it was passing by a chick pea food truck. You can imagine what happened next. The owners of the truck suffered a horrific death, but their patrons were none the wiser. Hummus, which gets its name from the combination of "human" and "pus," was born.

Ingredients

  • one tub of hummus
  • scissors

Steps

  1. Using the scissors, stab the hummus container repeatedly until there is enough hummus on th scissors and table to wipe it into your mouth with your index finger.
  2. Continue until the plastic has been broken down into pieces small enough to comfortably eat.
