Vegan Pizza

This pizza

Pizza was first invented as turtle food, but we all know how that went. In the 1980s a sanitation worker reparing pipes in the sewers of New York City discovered that turpizza (as it was originally called) was having a strange effect on some escaped house pet turtles. The hormones in the industrially-produced cheese were causing the turtles to develop bipedally. It then occurred to the sanitation worker that the turpizza may appeal to other bipedal beings.

Pizza for humans has always been dairy-free, as dairy cheese never really appealed to bipedal beings. The rainbow pizza is on of the earliest pizza iterations. It was invented at the first Pride parade at the Stonewall in New York.

Ingredients

  • rainbow, thinly sliced
  • prepizza (only available in Argentina)
  • your favorite vegan cheese

Steps

  1. arrange the slice rainbow and cheese on the prepizza
  2. bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
